Project Modules

This project has declared the following modules:

Name Description
Power TAC server interface Interfaces and domain types shared by multiple server components
Power TAC accounting service Processes transactions from customers, brokers, and the market
Power TAC wholesale market Simulation of day-ahead wholesale market for trading electric power
Power TAC intra-hour market-based balancing Balances supply and demand in the current timeslot
Power TAC customer models Set of bottom-up customer implementations
Power TAC ev customer Bottom-up model of an electric vehicle customer
Power TAC default broker Simulates holder of all customer tariffs prior to market opening
Power TAC distribution utility Models the regulated monopoly that owns and maintains the distribution infrastructure.
Power TAC factored customer Top-down statistical model framework for different classes of customers.
Power TAC genco Models of simple wholesale power producers, and a wholesale buyer
Power TAC household customer Bottom-up model of a household energy customer
Power TAC office complex customer Bottom-up model of an office complex energy customer
Power TAC logtool core Tool for analyzing state logs produced by the Power TAC server
Power TAC server main Top-level module of the Power TAC simulation server
Power TAC visualizer v2 Properties, plugin- and dependency management for Power TAC
